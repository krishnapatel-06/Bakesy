Our Story

Founded in 2026, Bakesy began as a small experimental kitchen dedicated to the forgotten art of long-fermentation baking. We believe that modern speed is the enemy of flavor.

That's why our kitchen never sleeps; we work with the rhythms of nature, allowing our sourdoughs to rest for 48 hours and our chocolates to be tempered in small, precise batches.
